Clima Andes

Precision Meteorological Forecasting for High-Altitude Operations & Ecosystem Resilience.

Our Mission

Leveraging hyper-local data and advanced atmospheric modeling, Clima Andes provides critical climate intelligence to sectors navigating the unique challenges of the Andean Altiplano. We translate complex climate data into actionable decisions, supporting both economic vitality and ecological conservation.

Core Focus Areas

Agriculture

Providing precision frost forecasting, irrigation scheduling, and pest risk analysis to optimize yields for Andean farmers.

Tourism

Enhancing safety and visitor experience through hyper-local weather intelligence for high-altitude trekking and observatory operations.

About the Founder

Founded by a climatologist with extensive experience at the ALMA Observatory, Clima Andes is built on a foundation of rigorous scientific research and local Andean knowledge.
